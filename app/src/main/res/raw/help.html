What is a synonym?

If two or more words have the same meaning in a specific context, they are synonyms.
Examples:
  • Adresse, Anschrift
  • gelingen, glücken, klappen
  • oft, häufig

Whether two words are synonymous can be easily tested with a made-up sentence like Ich gehe oft ins Kino. In this case, oft can be replaced by häufig without changing the meaning of the sentence. Hence, oft and häufig are synonyms.

The following pairs of words, on the other hand, are not synonymous:
  • warm, heiß (the meaning differs too much)
  • Haus, Gebäude (Haus is a hyponym of Gebäude, not a synonym)

Synonyms of the same meaning form a synonym group. A word with several meanings – e.g. Bank – will appear in as many synonym groups as it has meanings:

Synonym group 1: Bank, Kreditinstitut
Synonym group 2: Bank, Sitzbank
