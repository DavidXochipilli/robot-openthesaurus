OpenThesaurus

OpenThesaurus is a German dictionary for synonyms and associations. It can be used for looking up words with the same or a similar meaning. For example, searching for falsch will return, among others, the synonyms inkorrekt, unrichtig, verkehrt.

Everybody can participate and fix errors in OpenThesaurus or add new synonyms. The search interface will show all meanings of a word (e.g. roh -> roh, ungekocht and another entry for roh, rau, grob, unsanft).
